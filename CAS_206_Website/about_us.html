About Our Bakery

The founder made the decision to open the doors in 1975 after graduating college with a business degree and a love for baking. From there many hours have been made to make The Nines Bakery one of the best bakeries in Portland, Or

We now currently employ 24 bakery professionals. Supporting their families and offering scholarships to Culinary school for under-privileged youth.

Community involvement is about of our mission statement and twice a year we over "Muffins to the Homeless" where we make sure the homeless in this great state of Oregon are feed for a day. We also run fundraiser for the local food banks. Actually 5% of every transaction goes to Feeding the poor in Oregon.

So come make your family apart of our family and join the long tradion of Nines Bakery in Portland.
